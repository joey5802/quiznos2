The Quiznos Employee Training game was used by Quiznos in the last decade to train employees on working for the company. Employees would be loaned a custom Nintendo Game Boy and told that their employment depended on the time in which they could finish the game.

 

The video game was a part of Project Luxury, spearheaded by head of marketing Paul Roberts. It was recently discontinued internally due to a strange recurring glitch that was "scaring off employees."
